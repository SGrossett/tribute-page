It's okay to change

"Just be yourself" is a phrase often heard at moments when you are in some sort of transition. Almost as if to say certain change is fine but not when it's to better yourself. However, not only is change okay, it is practically inevitable. People don't feel, think, or believe the same way they did when they were 4, 18, 25, or even 60 years old. In the episode Greg the Babysitter, Rose Quartz expresses:

"When a Gem is made, it's for a reason. They burst out of the ground already knowing what they're supposed to be, and then... that's what they are. Forever. But you, you're supposed to change. You're never the same even moment to moment; you're allowed and expected to invent who you are. What an incredible power, the ability to 'grow up.'".
What's left to say? (Episode: Greg the Babysitter number)

Perfectly Imperfect

It's okay to make mistakes. Really. It can be hard to recognize at the moment, but your flaws do not define you nor do they take away from what makes you great. Like most of us, Steven Universe is far from perfect and he often makes mistakes. But with the help from the ones he loves, he is able to learn from them and grow as a person and gem. Your mistakes can also serve as excellent learning tools if you are will to approach them with even just a smidgeon of optimism. Greg Universe has a fan-favourite catchphrase in the show that goes, "If every porkchop were perfect, we wouldn't have hotdogs". Although the saying is about food, the lesson can be applied to all aspects of life. You can still make something good come out of a bad situation. Like transforming an imperfect pork chop into a delicious hotdog. (Episode number)
